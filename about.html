About This Site

Built by Someone Who
Knows the Industry.

Property Owner Coverage was created to give landlords and real estate investors the same insurance knowledge that agents use every day — in plain English, for free.

"Most property owners don't find out they're underinsured until they have a claim. This site exists to change that."

Why We Built This

After 20+ years placing commercial insurance for apartment owners, landlords, and real estate investors across California, one thing became clear: most property owners are making expensive coverage decisions with almost no reliable information.

They're relying on whatever their lender requires, whatever their current agent recommends, or whatever they've always done. Meanwhile, the insurance market has changed dramatically — rates are up, carriers have pulled back, and the gaps in standard policies have never been more dangerous.

Property Owner Coverage was built to level the playing field. Every guide, tool, and resource on this site reflects real-world commercial insurance knowledge — not generic content written by people who've never seen a policy declaration page.

Areas of Expertise

Apartment Buildings

10-unit to 100+ unit multifamily. Garden style, mid-rise, value-add acquisitions, and stabilized assets.

Commercial Real Estate

Mixed-use, retail strips, office buildings, and owner-occupied commercial properties across California.

California Market

Deep knowledge of the CA hard market — wildfire exposure, FAIR Plan alternatives, admitted vs. E&S placement.

Coverage Gaps

Habitability, building ordinance/law, coinsurance, wrongful eviction, and loss of rents — the issues that matter most at claim time.

How the Site Works

Articles are written from the perspective of a commercial insurance specialist with two decades of California experience. They cover the questions real clients ask — not just textbook definitions.

The free tools — including the Policy Analyzer, Premium Estimator, and Coinsurance Calculator — are built to give property owners real answers, not marketing fluff.

Nothing on this site is sponsored or paid for by any insurance company or carrier. The goal is honest, useful information.
