Privacy Policy

How we handle information on PropertyOwnerCoverage.com.

What We Collect

PropertyOwnerCoverage.com does not require account creation or registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or personal information unless you voluntarily contact us.

When you use the Policy Analyzer tool, uploaded documents are sent to our AI processing service for analysis. Documents are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers after analysis is complete.

Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, we may collect standard web analytics data including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and general geographic location (country/region level). This data is aggregated and anonymous — it cannot be used to identify individual visitors.

We use this data solely to understand how visitors use the site and to improve content and tools.

Cookies

This site may use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. We do not use cookies for advertising or to track users across other websites. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate this site, including hosting (Vercel) and AI processing (Anthropic). These services have their own privacy policies. Uploaded documents sent to the Policy Analyzer are processed via Anthropic's API under their data handling terms.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Advertising

This site displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads.
